About

Hi, I'm Alex. I'm a researcher on the Safeguards team at UK AISI, where I work on the safety and security of frontier LLMs. I've contributed to several pre-deployment evaluations and red-teaming efforts focused on frontier LLM's misuse safeguards and alignment (see Anthropic and OpenAI blogposts), and worked on popular open source evals such as StrongReject and AgentHarm. I'm currently working on understanding data poisoning and building misalignment evals. Previously, I studied Machine Learning at UCL and Maths at Cambridge, interned at CHAI, and in a previous life worked at Microsoft as a SWE. Feel free to drop me an email!
